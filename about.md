# VersusMode by Gha16

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
**OVERVIEW**
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

VersusMode brings a dedicated **1v1 competitive experience** to Geometry Dash 2.2. Powered by the Globed API, you can now challenge an opponent to a real-time duel on any rated level!

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
**HOW TO PLAY**
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

1. **The Hub**: Click the "Sword Icon" on the main menu.
2. **Matchmaking**: Ensure you are exactly **two players** in a Globed room. A 15-second countdown will begin.
3. **Level Selection**: Both players can vote for a Level ID. If votes differ, the system automatically synchronizes both players to the same level.
4. **The Duel**: Once the timer hits zero, the level launches instantly for both competitors.
5. **Victory**: Be the first to reach **20 points**! Points are earned based on the Star rating of the levels you complete.

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
**FEATURES**
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

* **Strict 1v1**: Optimized for head-to-head races with real-time opponent icons.
* **Smart Sync**: Both players are guaranteed to play the exact same level.
* **Official & Custom**: Race through Main levels, The Tower, or any online Level ID.
* **Real Rewards**: Earn Stars, Orbs, and Diamonds on your official profile while you compete.

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
**REQUIREMENTS**
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

* **Geode**: v5.6.1 or newer.
* **Globed**: Must be in a room with exactly **one other player**.
* **Node IDs**: Required for menu integration.
